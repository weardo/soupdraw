SoupDraw

Draw on your camera with your hands.

Annotate any video call with hand gestures. Everything runs on your device: no video, no frames, nothing ever leaves your machine.

▶ Practice the gestures

Three steps to your first drawing

  1. Turn it on. Click the SoupDraw icon in your toolbar and switch on Augment my camera.
  2. Join a call (Meet, Zoom web, Teams, Whereby…) and pick your normal camera. The call now receives your augmented feed.
  3. Arm and draw. Press Alt+Shift+D to arm, then pinch your thumb and index finger to draw. Press it again before you talk.

Your gestures

Rebind any gesture from the toolbar popup, under Gesture controls.

Call-safe by design

Gestures are only read when armed. Disarm (Alt+Shift+D) before you speak, so nothing is interpreted while you gesture naturally. When the extension is off, apps get your real camera, untouched.
