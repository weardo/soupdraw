# SoupDraw — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-07-15_

**SoupDraw does not collect, transmit, store remotely, sell, or share any data.
Everything happens on your device.**

## Camera

SoupDraw augments the camera feed that a website you visit requests. It reads
video frames **only in your browser**, on your device, to:

- run an on-device hand-tracking model (bundled with the extension; no network),
- composite your gesture drawings onto a local canvas, and
- return that canvas as the camera stream to the site you chose.

No video, no frames, no images, and no hand data ever leave your machine. There
are **no network requests at runtime** of any kind.

## Storage

Your settings and your current drawing are saved **locally** in the browser
(`storage.local`) so they persist between sessions. This never leaves your device
and is removed if you uninstall the extension.

## No tracking

No analytics, no telemetry, no accounts, no cookies, no third-party services.

## Permissions

- **storage** — to remember your settings/drawing locally (above).
- Host access (`<all_urls>`) and the camera interception exist so the extension
  can work inside any video-call site you use. It only ever composites a local
  canvas; it does not read or send your data anywhere.
